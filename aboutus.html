TIMELINE

THE IDEA: CHALALA and GATATA, two close friends, often dreamed of starting a business together. One day,they decided to turn their ideas into reality and embarked on this exciting journey.

PLANNING: They met frequently to brainstorm and plan. Over coffee, they sketched out ideas, discussing everything from products to marketing strategies. However, their initial meetings were filled with confusion and disagreements.

FAIL: Despite their enthusiasm, many of their plans fell apart. They faced obstacles that made them question their partnership, leading to frustration and uncertainty about their future.

SUCCESS: Undeterred, they learned to communicate better and refined their vision. Finally, when they launched their online store, it quickly gained popularity. Their hard work paid off, and their friendship grew even stronger through the challenges they overcame.
